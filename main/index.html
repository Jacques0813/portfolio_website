Colour tracking system

The insparation behind this was pretty simple. A youtuber by the name of Micheal Reeves made his goldfish buy stocks for him by viewing stocks either on the right or left side of the tank and determining the stock to buy by what area his fish spent the most time within a 5 minute time frame. This was done by a color tracker so I tried it myself because I always wanted to know how hardware gets connected with code. This example tracks the largest concentration of red, and orange being the largest concentration here gets marked with a box.

Monitoring system on a web server

I was fortunate enough to partake in Belgium Campus's international project for the year 2023. We were working together with Lockheed Martin (famous for making an AI piloted fighter jet that managed to pilot without error for 17 hours) and Pennstate University from Pennsylvania and were tasked to create a hybrid powering system for a UAV (a drone) by sharing the power load between a battery and RC plane motor with the hopes of extending flight time. Our job was to aid the technical side (Pennsate) in monitoring various measurements throughout the system for testing purposes and that is what you see here. This example is imported locally, but the actual version runs on a web server with node-red installed.
